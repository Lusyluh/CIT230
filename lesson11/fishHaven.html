Ways to gift memories instead of objects this Christmas!

By Charlotte Gelstharp

When you think about your Christmas growing up what do you remember the most? The memories that seem to last the longest for most of us are those great family Christmas traditions.

Christmas has always been a special time of year, but when you have little ones they add an extra special bit of sparkle to the festive season. Their excitement and happiness is contagious making it hard not to get carried away and have a Christmas present bonanza.

But memories and traditions are the things that children will remember long after their gifts have been unwrapped. These are the things that last a lifetime and make Christmas special.

Most people have things that they did year after year that have stayed with you as fond memories. Small, low cost but fun things such as going to choose the tree together or wearing ugly Christmas jumpers to watch your fave Christmas film, make perfect activities that can easily become fond traditions. Don't underestimate the value of time spent together enjoying each other's company, it's amazing how much children remember family traditions.
