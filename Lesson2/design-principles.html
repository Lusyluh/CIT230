Design Principles

White Space

The Nike website has a lot of white space in between the products, the images are clear and have white space in between that makes the page appear nice and clean.

Alignment

Facebook.com

Facebook shows a good design principle of aignment. The live stories are aligned nicely at the top, creating a nice visual to the website. Important links like the news feed are aligned to the left of the page, while all advertisements are aligned to the right.

Hick's Law

BYUI

The university website is a good example of Hick's Law applied. It take a few minutes for a student or visitor to the page to get what they want. There aren't many options to confuse the viewer.
